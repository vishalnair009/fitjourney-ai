# Component: Primary Button

## Purpose

Primary action button used throughout the application.

---

## Variants

* Enabled
* Disabled
* Loading

---

## Size

Height: 56px

Width: Full Width

Radius: 28px

---

## Colors

Enabled:
Background: Emerald Green (#2E8B57)

Disabled:
Background: #D1D5DB

Loading:
Background: Emerald Green

---

## Typography

Font: Inter

Weight: SemiBold

Size: 18px

Color: White

---

## Behaviour

Enabled

* Tap → Next action

Disabled

* No interaction

Loading

* Spinner appears
* User cannot tap again

---

## Accessibility

Minimum tap size: 48px

High contrast text

Keyboard accessible

Screen reader label required
