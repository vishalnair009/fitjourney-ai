# ADR-001 - Journey First Experience

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

FitJourney AI will be a Journey-First application.

The home screen will represent the user's transformation journey rather than a traditional dashboard.

## Reason

People stay motivated by seeing progress toward meaningful goals rather than viewing isolated metrics.

The Journey Timeline will become the central experience of the application.

## Consequences

Every future feature should support and enhance the user's journey.
