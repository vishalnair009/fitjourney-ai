# ADR-003 – Drona Visual Identity

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Drona will have a friendly illustrated avatar throughout the application.

The avatar will act as a companion, not as the main focus of the interface.

## Reason

Research shows users build stronger emotional connections with products that have a recognizable personality.

A simple illustration creates familiarity without making the experience childish or distracting.

## Design Principles

* Modern
* Friendly
* Minimal
* Trustworthy
* Calm
* Professional

## Future

The avatar may display different expressions based on the user's journey:

😊 Celebrating success

🤔 Thinking

💪 Motivating

🎉 Milestone achieved

❤️ Encouraging after setbacks
