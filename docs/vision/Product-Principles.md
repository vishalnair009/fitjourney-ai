# Product Principles

## Principle 1 - Guide, Don't Track

FitJourney AI exists to guide users throughout their fitness journey, not merely collect data.

---

## Principle 2 - Every Screen Must Motivate

Every screen should inspire the user to continue their journey.

---

## Principle 3 - The Journey Is the Product

The transformation journey is the central experience of the application.

---

## Principle 4 - AI Learns Every User

The AI remembers habits, preferences, struggles, and achievements to provide personalized guidance.

---

## Principle 5 - Celebrate Progress

Small achievements deserve recognition.

---

## Principle 6 - Simplicity Wins

Users should always know the next best action.

---

## Principle 7 - Science Over Trends

Recommendations should be based on evidence and realistic expectations, not fad diets or unrealistic promises.

---

## Principle 8 - Sustainable Fitness

The goal is to build lifelong healthy habits, not short-term transformations.
